What SWUTrade is

SWUTrade is a free web app and Discord bot that helps players of Star Wars Unlimited coordinate trades. It builds and shares trade proposals, settles live shared trades between two players, surfaces matches between users in the same Discord communities, and tracks each user's wishlist and trade binder.

SWUTrade does not escrow cards, hold money, ship cards, or guarantee that any trade actually happens. The actual exchange of physical or digital cards always happens between the two users — we just help them coordinate.

Trade execution and disputes

SWUTrade does not process payments, ship cards, or guarantee any trade. Once two users have agreed to a trade in the app, the actual exchange happens between them — by mail, in person at a local game store, or however they arrange. Disputes about the physical exchange (a card never arrived, condition was misrepresented, the other party stopped responding) are between the two users involved.

SWUTrade can record what was agreed (a settled trade's card list is preserved in the database) and that record can be used as a reference if you escalate to a third party. We do not act as an arbiter.
